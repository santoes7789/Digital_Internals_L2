Help

To use the timer, hold the space bar until the text turns green, then release it to start the timer. Press the space bar again to stop the timer, and it will automatically record your time.

You can view your session times in the table on right - you can scroll when it overflows!

You can also change the session type by clicking on the session name at the bottom left, and you can add new sessions by typing in the input box and clicking the plus button.

Empty sessions are automatically deleted upon refresh, so make sure to add a time.

Quickly delete the last time by pressing shift + backspace.

You can also view your statistics by clicking on the "Statistics" link in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
